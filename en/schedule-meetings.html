Our Services

AI that Schedules Meetings for You.

Stop wasting hours on email back-and-forths to find a time. Our AI agent integrates with your calendar and manages the entire scheduling process, 24/7.

More than a Calendly. A Personal Assistant.

24/7 Smart Scheduling

The AI chats with your client via email or chat, understands their availability, and cross-references it with your calendar to find the perfect time.

Calendar Management

Syncs with Google Calendar, Outlook, etc. It manages multiple calendars, avoids conflicts, and automatically considers time zones.

Follow-ups and Reminders

Sends automatic reminders to all participants to reduce no-shows and confirmations to ensure everyone is aligned.

Integrated Qualification

Before scheduling, the AI can ask qualifying questions to ensure the meeting is with the right person and worth your time.

Our Implementation Process

1

Diagnosis

We connect to your calendars and understand your scheduling rules (duration, buffers, preferred times).

2

Customization

We configure the agent's "personality," email/message templates, and qualification rules.

3

Integration

We activate the agent in your email, website, or WhatsApp. It starts managing incoming scheduling requests.

4

Optimization

We analyze interactions and refine the rules to make the process increasingly efficient and natural.
