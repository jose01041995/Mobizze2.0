Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Last updated: May 24, 2024.

1. Introduction

AbsolutAI ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website [absolutai.com] and use our services.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect information about you in various ways:

  • Personal Data: Personally identifiable information, such as your name, address, email, and telephone number, that you voluntarily give to us when you fill out contact forms or communicate with us.
  • Usage Data: Information your browser automatically sends whenever you visit our site, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the time spent on those pages.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and manage our services.
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests.
  • Send you newsletters, marketing materials, and other information that may be of interest to you.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends for business purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties for their marketing purposes without your consent. We may share information with service providers who assist us in operating our business (e.g., website hosting, data analysis), who are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

5. Security of Your Data

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Your Rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, and restrict the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at contato@absolutai.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date of this policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contato@absolutai.com.
