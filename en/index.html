Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About AI Agents and Automation

What is an AI agent? +
It is intelligent software that autonomously executes tasks — answers messages, qualifies leads, processes data, makes simple decisions. It works 24/7, learns from your business context, and integrates into your existing systems.
What is the difference between automation and AI? +
Automation follows fixed rules. AI interprets and adapts. With AbsolutAI, we combine both to create perfect and dynamic workflows for your company.
Do I need a technical team to use AI? +
No. We design everything for your team to use without technical training. We provide documentation, do onboarding, and offer continuous support.
How long does implementation take? +
It depends on complexity, but most of our basic agents (chatbots, qualification bots) are up and running in 2 to 4 weeks. More complex deep integration systems may take between 6 to 8 weeks.
Will AI replace my team? +
No. The goal of AbsolutAI is to free your employees from repetitive and monotonous tasks (like processing data or answering the same questions dozens of times). Thus, your team focuses on strategic, creative work and relationships that generate more value.
How do you guarantee data security and privacy? +
We take security very seriously. We use secure infrastructures in compliance with the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation). Your company and client data is never shared with third parties or used to train public AI models.
Does your AI integrate with the software we already use (CRM, ERP)? +
Yes! We are integration specialists. We connect AI agents to your current software (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Shopify, billing software, etc.) so that data flows invisibly and you don't have to change the way your company already works.
In what languages can the agents operate? +
Since we operate in Portugal and Worldwide, our agents are prepared to act natively in dozens of languages (Portuguese, English, Spanish, French, etc.), perfectly understanding the context and nuances of each language.
What is the cost of the service? +
The investment depends on your specific needs. We offer a 100% free diagnosis where we map your current process and present a plan with the estimated ROI (Return on Investment). Most of our clients recover their investment quickly through the hours saved.
